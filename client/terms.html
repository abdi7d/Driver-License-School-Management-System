Terms of Service

These terms describe how the Driver License School Management System is used by students, instructors, supervisors, and managers.

Use of the system

Users must provide accurate registration details, keep credentials secure, and use the platform only for legitimate training, scheduling, reporting, and certification activities.

Account approval

Student accounts require approval before access is granted. Role-based access controls restrict each portal to the correct workflow and permissions.

Data responsibility

All training records, exam results, attendance logs, and certificates must be maintained accurately by authorized staff.
