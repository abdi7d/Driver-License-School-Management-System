Enrollment Process

A structured pathway from registration to certification

01

Registration

Submit your application online with required documents and select your preferred license level.

02

Approval

Administrative review and verification of submitted credentials by management team.

03

Theory Training

Comprehensive classroom instruction covering traffic regulations and road safety principles.

04

Practical Training

Supervised driving sessions with certified instructors using modern training vehicles.

05

Examination

Written and practical assessments conducted by authorized examiners to evaluate competency.

06

Certification

Upon successful completion, receive your official driving certificate and license eligibility.
