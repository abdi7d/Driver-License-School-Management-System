Privacy Policy

This policy explains how the Driver License School Management System stores and protects account, training, exam, and certification data.

What we collect

We store registration details, training progress, attendance, exam results, uploaded documents, notifications, and certificates necessary to operate the school workflow.

How data is used

Data is used to manage enrollment, assignments, training progress, approvals, certification, reporting, and notifications.

Access control

Only authenticated users with the appropriate role can access their permitted information. Sensitive actions are logged and protected by the backend authorization layer.
